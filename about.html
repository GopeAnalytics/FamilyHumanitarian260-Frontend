About Family Humanitarian Organisation

Bringing clean water and sanitation to underserved communities across Africa, one well at a time.

Our Mission

The Family Humanitarian Organisation (FH260) is dedicated to addressing one of the most fundamental human rights: access to clean water. Our mission is to transform lives in underserved communities across Africa by providing sustainable water solutions through well drilling projects and sanitation initiatives.

Since our founding, we have focused on creating lasting change in Kenya, Uganda, Ghana and with advancement into Zambia and Tanzania soon, empowering communities to break the cycle of water scarcity and build healthier futures. We believe that water is the foundation of community development, enabling education, health, and economic growth.

Through partnerships with local leaders and communities, we ensure that each project is sustainable, culturally appropriate, and managed by those it serves. Our holistic approach combines infrastructure development with education and training, creating lasting solutions to water challenges.
